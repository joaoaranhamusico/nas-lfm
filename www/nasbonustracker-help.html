NAS Bonus Tracker — Quick Guide

How to save your progress, export/import backups, and reset your week.
Back to App

What it does

For all NAS users

The NAS Weekly Bonus Tracker helps you tick off daily/weekly bonuses, see your running total, and track your current tier.

Where your data is stored

Your progress is saved locally in your browser using localStorage. It is not stored on a server.

If you clear your browser data, switch browsers or devices, your progress will be removed unless you create a backup first.

Show times & your timezone

Program times (e.g., Mr. Oddzo Show, Top 20 Show) are displayed automatically in your local timezone. The “Starts in” countdown is also adjusted to your timezone.

Export (save a backup)

  1. Click Export (top right).
  2. A file named like nas_bonus_YYYY-MM-DD.json will be downloaded.
  3. Keep it in a safe place (you can rename it if you want).

Tip: export before clearing cookies/cache, changing devices, or moving to a different browser.

Import (restore a backup)

  1. Click Import.
  2. Select your previously saved .json file.
  3. Your progress, checkboxes and totals will be restored exactly as they were at export time.

Reset Week

Use Reset Week to clear the current week and start fresh. This does not touch your backup files; it only resets what’s in your browser.

Good practices

  • Export regularly (weekly or after major updates).
  • Keep a single source of truth for your last backup (e.g., a NAS folder or cloud drive).
  • If you use multiple devices, import the latest backup before continuing.

FAQ

Q: I can’t see my previous points.
A: You likely cleared browser data or switched devices. Use Import with your last .json backup.

Q: Can I sync between devices automatically?
A: Not at this time. Use Export/Import to move your progress between devices.

Q: Are my files shared with NAS?
A: No. Backups remain with you. The app doesn’t upload your data.

Troubleshooting

  • If Import fails, make sure the file is a valid .json created by the app.
  • After importing, refresh the page once if the UI does not update immediately.
  • Confirm your system timezone is correct if the show times look off.
